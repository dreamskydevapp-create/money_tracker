## How to delete your account

### Option 1: Delete directly inside the app
1. Open **Money Tracker - Budget Planner**
2. Go to **Profile** tab
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

---

### Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, send an email to:

**dreamskydev.app@gmail.com**

**Subject:** `Account Deletion Request – Money Tracker`

**Include in the email:**
- The email address linked to your account
- Your request to delete your account and all data

We will process your request within **7 business days**.

---

## What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

| Data | Deleted? |
|---|---|
| Account (email, user ID) | ✅ Yes |
| All transactions | ✅ Yes |
| Wallets and balances | ✅ Yes |
| Budgets | ✅ Yes |
| App settings and preferences | ✅ Yes |
| Anonymous analytics data | ❌ Retained (not linked to your identity) |
